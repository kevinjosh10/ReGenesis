# Project Name

Alternative tagline:

# Vision

The world generates billions of tons of waste every year. Most organizations still view waste as an unavoidable expense that must be disposed of. This mindset overlooks a significant opportunity: many waste materials retain economic value and can serve as inputs for new products and businesses.

ReGenesis exists to change that perspective.

Rather than treating waste as the end of a product's lifecycle, ReGenesis treats it as the beginning of the next one. The platform enables organizations to identify hidden value in discarded materials, discover manufacturing opportunities, estimate profitability, and support better business decisions.

ReGenesis is not a waste management system.

It is an **Opportunity Discovery Platform** for the circular economy.

---

# Mission

To help organizations unlock economic value from waste by transforming discarded materials into resources, products, and sustainable business opportunities through intelligent decision support.

---

# Problem Statement

Industries, institutions, and municipalities generate large amounts of waste every day.

Most organizations know:

* How much waste they generate.
* How much is recycled.
* How much goes to landfill.

But they rarely know:

* What resources they already own.
* Which waste streams have the highest business potential.
* Which products can be manufactured from those materials.
* Which option provides the highest return on investment.
* How much wealth could be generated from those resources.

Existing waste software focuses on tracking and reporting.

Very few systems focus on **creating economic value**.

---

# Solution

ReGenesis transforms waste into a resource inventory and analyzes it to discover opportunities for manufacturing and entrepreneurship.

Instead of answering:

> "How should this waste be disposed of?"

It answers:

> "What can this become?"

The platform provides:

* Resource valuation
* Product discovery
* Opportunity ranking
* Business model generation
* Revenue estimation
* Sustainability impact
* Executive decision support

# Purpose

The Opportunity Engine is the intelligence layer of ReGenesis.

It analyzes the organization's available resources and answers one question:

> **"What is the highest-value opportunity hidden inside these resources?"**

Unlike traditional waste management software, it does not simply classify waste or recommend recycling.

It identifies products, compares manufacturing possibilities, estimates revenue, ranks opportunities, and explains every recommendation transparently.

Traditional systems ask:

ReGenesis asks:

This small shift completely changes the experience.

# рҹҡЁ One Major Improvement (Replacing Drag & Drop)

After thinking more deeply, **I would actually change this feature**.

Dragging resources looks cool, but for an industry user it's slower than necessary.

Instead, I'd implement a **Resource Mixer**:

* Each resource is shown as a selectable chip or card.
* Click resources to add/remove them.
* Selected resources appear in a horizontal "mixing tray."
* A single **Generate Opportunities** button analyzes the combination.

Example:

```text
Available Resources

вҳ‘ Plastic
вҳ‘ Wood
вҳҗ Glass
вҳ‘ Metal

Selected Mix

Plastic + Wood + Metal

[ Generate Opportunities ]
```

Why I prefer this:

* Faster than drag-and-drop.
* Easier on mobile.
* Simpler to implement.
* More accessible.
* Still visually appealing.

You can still add subtle animations (cards sliding into the tray, glowing connectors, smooth transitions) without the complexity of a full drag-and-drop system.

## My recommendation

I would **replace drag-and-drop with the Resource Mixer**. It keeps the "crafting" concept while making the product feel more practical and polished.
